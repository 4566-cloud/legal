We collect your dietary preferences to generate customized meal plans. ONLY the AI-generated recipe steps are securely saved to our cloud servers; all other personal preference data is stored locally on your device.
Special Notice on Photo Data: When you use the photo-to-calorie recognition feature, your photos are securely transmitted to our third-party provider (Tongyi Qianwen Model) for real-time visual analysis. Once the analysis is complete, the photo data is immediately destroyed. We NEVER store your photos on our servers, and they are used solely for providing the calorie recognition service and for no other purposes.
